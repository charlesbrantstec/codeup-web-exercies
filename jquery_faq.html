National Parks FAQ's:

What was the first national park?
Yellowstone National Park

When was the first national founded?
Yellowstone National Park is the first national park and was founded in 1872.

Which president established the National Park Service?
President Woodrow Wilson established the National Park Service in 1916.

Is the Grand Canyon the most visited national park?
No, the most visited national park is the Great Smoky Mountains National Park

Among the national parks how popular is the Grand Canyon?
The Grand Canyon is the 2nd most visited national park.

How many national park visits were there in 2019?
327,516,619

What is the parent agency of the National Park Service?
The Department of the Interior.

When was the Redwood National Park founded?
Redwood National Park was founded in 1968.

How many total visits to national parks have there been in the U.S. since their conception?
14,654,346,148

Which parks had more than 10 million visits last year?
Golden Gate National Recreation Area, Blue Ridge Parkway, and Great Smoky Mountains National Park
